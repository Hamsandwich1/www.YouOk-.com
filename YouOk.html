You Ok?

This is a safe space designed to help people with any problems or concerns they are struggling with, You OK? is tailor made for people who find themselves being constantly held back and need help to move foreward.

All it takes is one question to make someone's day a little better, so ask them: You Ok?

Exercising

~ Getting any kind of excercise is not only good for your physical health, but your mental health. Anywhere from an hour at the gym to a 10 minute walk will put you in a better mood

Sleep

~Getting the right amount of sleep each night is very important for the functionality of your brain, a lack of sleep could lead to you not thinking straight or being anxious

Eat Well

~Your diet has a direct impact on your mood, eating takeaways and other bad foods all the time will have a negative effect on your physical and mental health. Eat those greens!

Socialise

~It is important to see frinds and family regularly

Reading

Reading can help you to relax and activate your brain

Relax

~Putting the phone down for a while and simply doing nothing is one of the best ways to look after your mental health
